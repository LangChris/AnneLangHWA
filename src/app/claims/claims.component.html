Claims

As we are all currently witnessing, the circumstances surrounding COVID-19 pandemic are changing by the hour. For this very reason, it is essential during this unprecedented time that we band together as a community – remaining steadfast in our commitment to the health and wellbeing of homeowners, our service contractors and our staff.

As a valued customer and member of the HWA community, your safety and peace of mind are of the utmost important to us. Due to government health and safety directives and/or other events beyond our control related to COVID-19, we are currently prioritizing high-risk, emergency claims, such as total loss of air conditioning/ heat emergencies in extreme weather, inability to have access to running water or excessive water leak that cannot be shut off.

We also want to assure you that all customer claims are important to us and we will do our best to resolve all other claims as soon as we possibly can; however, some customers may experience service delays. Current or future government restrictions in some areas may prevent service providers from promptly responding to claims.

Steps you can take:

  • For your convenience, we encourage you to visit our online self-service HWA Home Warranty customer portal available here 24/7 to help you manage your account anytime, anywhere. It's your one-stop shop to make a payment, file a claim, identify the contractor assigned to your claim, view contractor contact information and more.
  • If you need to file a claim, please visit our website at www.hwahomewarranty.com. If you are using our customer portal for the first time, you will need to follow a few simple steps to establish an online account with us.
  • If you have a question regarding an existing claim, please email us using the subject line: Inquiry; Claim number SRO XXXXXXX.
  • Upcoming Appointments:
    • If you need to reschedule an upcoming appointment, please contact the contractor to reschedule for a later date. As call volumes may be high, please leave a voicemail if prompted.
    • If you're not feeling well and already have an in-home appointment scheduled, please contact the contractor as soon as possible to reschedule your appointment for a future date.
    • If you have an in-home appointment with a contractor and notice the contractor showing any signs of illness, you have the right to cancel the service and reschedule your appointment for another date. Please send us an email using the subject line: Reschedule Appt; Claim number SRO XXXXXXX so we can document what happened and address the concern right away.
    • If you want to place your claim on hold till after the COVID-19 situation improves, please send us an email using the subject line: Requesting claim on hold due to COVID-19; Claim number SRO XXXXXXX. You can remove the hold at any time by emailing us using the subject line: Requesting HOLD Removal; Claim number SRO XXXXXXX.
  • Lastly, we encourage you to stay vigilant in washing your hands, practicing social distancing and educating yourself on the virus and how it spreads by visiting the CDC website at www.cdc.gov.

We truly appreciate your patience and understanding at this critical time, and we are committed and prepared to keep you informed about how we will adapt as the situation evolves.

Thank you for being a valued customer to HWA – you truly are at the heart of everything we do.
